With great enthusiasm, we announce the establishment of **Camelid Research Studies APS**, a non-profit association created to unite scientific research, veterinary medicine, and animal welfare in the world of domestic South American camelids.

In the coming months, the first projects for 2026–2027 will begin, starting with the study on the emotional baseline of alpacas and llamas. We will continue to publish updates on our activities, events, and outreach initiatives here.
